Methane & Its Role

Methane (CH4) is a colorless, odorless, flammable gas that is the primary component of natural gas. Methane is the simplest hydrocarbon, consisting of one carbon atom and four hydrogen atoms. Methane is a powerful greenhouse gas. It is used primarily as fuel to make heat and light. It is also used to manufacture organic chemicals.
